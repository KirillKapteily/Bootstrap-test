ASUS TUF Gaming F15

Aspire 7 is a compact laptop with a high-quality Full HD IPS display and a rich set of interfaces. Thanks to the high-performance hardware, the laptop will perfectly cope with the most demanding tasks, and is also perfect for modern games on high settings (e.g. Dota 2, CS:GO, Fortnite, GTA V, etc.). Stylish minimalist case with a metal cover Intel Core i5-12450H processor (8 cores/12 threads) and NVIDIA GeForce RTX 2050 4 GB graphics card 16 GB of RAM and PCIe 512 GB SDD drive (maximum RAM is 32 GB) 15.6" FullHD display with IPS matrix HD webcam 720p Two built-in microphones Amber backlit keyboard Up to 8 hours of work without recharging

Fantastic performance for gamers Rating: ★★★★★ (5/5) The ASUS TUF Gaming F15 offers fantastic performance for gamers on a budget! Equipped with the Intel Core i7 processor and NVIDIA RTX 3060, it runs demanding games like Cyberpunk 2077 and Apex Legends smoothly at high settings. The 144Hz FHD display provides a sharp, immersive experience, with no screen tearing or stutter during intense action sequences. It’s also great for multitasking, handling game streaming and content creation effortlessly. The cooling system works effectively, though it can get a bit loud under heavy load. For its price, the performance and gaming quality are exceptional—perfect for anyone who wants power without breaking the bank.

Really gaminig laptopRating: ★★★★☆ (4/5) The design of the ASUS TUF Gaming F15 is rugged and stylish, with a military-grade build that feels durable. I love the matte finish, and the keyboard has customizable RGB backlighting, which adds a nice gaming touch. The laptop is relatively slim for a gaming machine, but it’s still a bit bulky and on the heavier side at 2.3 kg, so it’s best suited for home or stationary use. The keys are comfortable for long gaming sessions, though the trackpad feels slightly less responsive. Overall, it’s built to last and has a distinct gamer aesthetic that looks great on any desk setup.

Battery Rating: ★★★☆☆ (3/5) While the ASUS TUF Gaming F15 performs incredibly well for gaming, its battery life leaves something to be desired. With regular browsing and productivity tasks, I get around 5 hours of battery life, but when gaming, it’s closer to 1–2 hours, so I keep it plugged in most of the time. The charging speed is decent, though, and the included power adapter is compact for a gaming laptop. Portability isn’t its strong suit, especially since it’s on the heavier side, but for the performance it offers, I think it’s worth the compromise. If you’re mainly using it at a desk, it’s an excellent choice.
