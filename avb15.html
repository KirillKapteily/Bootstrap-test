ASUS Vivobook 15

Asus laptops are distinguished by sophistication and style. It is known that the designers of this brand work diligently, creating products that satisfy demanding aesthetes. At the same time, Asus laptops are characterized by balanced ergonomics - among the presented models you can find many compact and portable devices that are popular among active users. In this list, the Vivobook 15 X1504 deserves attention, which combines power and an attractive design. Typical of ultrabooks, this laptop has a thin body with a smooth finish. The screen is 15 inches, with high resolution. With a weight of 1.7 kg, Vivobook 15 X1504 is easy to take with you on trips, vacations, or for daily activities, and it will also be a faithful assistant for students. The power of the laptop is impressive: the Intel Core i3-1215U processor provides enough resources to perform work tasks and projects. The amount of memory is enough for fast and efficient work using all the capabilities of the device. For fast data exchange with peripheral devices, Vivobook 15 offers a full set of interfaces, including one USB-C 3.2 Gen 1 port, two USB 3.2 Gen 1 Type-A ports and a USB 2.0 port. HDMI video output is intended for connecting external monitors or projectors. Vivobook 15 X1504 is an easy way to solve complex tasks. Simplify everyday tasks with the Vivobook 15, a stylish laptop ready to help anywhere, anytime.

Good performance Rating: ★★★★☆ (4/5) The ASUS Vivobook 15 offers solid performance for everyday tasks like browsing, streaming, and document editing. Powered by an Intel Core i5 processor and 8GB of RAM, it handles multitasking smoothly, even with multiple tabs and apps open. It’s not designed for heavy gaming or intensive workloads, but for standard use, it’s quick and responsive. The 512GB SSD allows for fast boot-up times and ample storage for general files. It’s a great laptop for students and professionals who need reliable performance without breaking the bank.

Display isn't very brightestRating: ★★★☆☆ (3/5) The 15.6-inch FHD display on the ASUS Vivobook 15 is decent for casual use, with good color accuracy, though it’s not the brightest screen. Working in brightly lit rooms can be a bit challenging as the screen’s brightness could be higher. The design, however, is sleek and modern, with a slim profile and lightweight build, making it easy to carry around. The ErgoLift hinge is a nice touch, slightly tilting the keyboard for better typing comfort. Overall, the display is average, but the slim, stylish design makes it a good choice for those who prioritize portability.

Good keyboard Rating: ★★★★☆ (4/5) One of the highlights of the ASUS Vivobook 15 is its comfortable keyboard, which is backlit and has a satisfying typing feel. It’s excellent for long typing sessions. The laptop includes a variety of ports: USB-C, USB-A, HDMI, and a microSD card reader, covering most connectivity needs. Battery life is reasonable, lasting around 6–7 hours on a single charge with regular usage, though it drains faster with heavier tasks. It’s a reliable machine with practical features, perfect for anyone who needs a laptop that balances functionality with a comfortable typing experience.
