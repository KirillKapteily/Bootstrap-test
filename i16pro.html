Excellent display Rating: ★★★★★ (5/5) The iPhone 16 Pro’s display is truly a game-changer! With its 6.1-inch ProMotion OLED screen, everything looks super smooth and vibrant. The refresh rate adjusts up to 120Hz, making scrolling and animations feel buttery smooth, and the HDR support is fantastic for watching videos and gaming. Apple’s A18 Bionic chip delivers flawless performance, handling everything from multitasking to graphics-heavy games without a hiccup. The new low-power mode is a welcome feature, extending battery life noticeably, especially with heavy use. Overall, the iPhone 16 Pro’s display and performance are top-notch, making it one of the best smartphones on the market.

Super CameraRating: ★★★★★ (5/5) The camera system on the iPhone 16 Pro is phenomenal! Apple really leveled up with the improved 48MP main sensor, which captures stunning detail and vibrant colors in both bright and low-light conditions. The new periscope-style telephoto lens allows for 6x optical zoom, which is perfect for getting clear shots from a distance without sacrificing quality. Night mode has also improved, with even more natural colors and reduced noise. For content creators and photography enthusiasts, this camera setup is a dream come true. My only minor gripe is that the ProRAW files take up a lot of storage, so I recommend getting a higher storage option if you take lots of high-res photos.

Good Rating: ★★★★☆ (4/5) Battery life on the iPhone 16 Pro is solid, lasting a full day on a single charge even with moderate to heavy usage. The MagSafe charging speeds have improved, which is convenient, but I still wish Apple would bring back faster wired charging options. Design-wise, it looks sleek with thinner bezels and the matte titanium frame that feels incredibly durable. The color options are stylish, especially the new Dark Blue variant. However, the iPhone 16 Pro is a bit heavier than previous models, which may take some getting used to. All in all, if battery life and build quality are important to you, this phone won’t disappoint.
